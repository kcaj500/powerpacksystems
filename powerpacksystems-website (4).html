What we install

Our
Services.

SERVICES
Service 01

Solar PV
Installation

Commercial, industrial and agricultural solar PV systems designed around your energy profile. We handle everything from feasibility and design through to DNO connection and commissioning — with MCS certification included.

We design both grid-tied and off-grid systems. Grid-tied installations export surplus generation for SEG income and retain full grid backup. Off-grid systems are designed for sites where a grid connection isn't viable — sized for fully independent operation, with optional generator integration.

What's included
Site survey and shading analysis
Full system design and yield estimate
Supply and installation of panels, inverters and mounting
G98/G99 DNO notification and connection
MCS certificate for SEG export payments
Monitoring system setup

Solar PV
Service 02

Battery Energy
Storage

Containerised BESS units using Tier 1 LiFePO4 battery cells and market-leading inverters. Store cheap overnight-rate power or excess solar generation and deploy it during peak periods — reducing your grid dependency and cutting energy costs.

What's included
Energy consumption and tariff analysis
Custom system design, sized to your demand and tariff
LiFePO4 battery modules with manufacturer warranty
Inverter stack — single-phase, split-phase or 3-phase
Remote monitoring and energy management system
Weatherproof shipping container
Full commissioning and handover documentation

DNO Upgrade

Grid connection upgrades are expensive, slow, and often unnecessary. No lengthy applications. No upgrade costs. Just a smarter system.

What's included
Peak demand analysis against your existing connection
System design to maximise power within current capacity
Battery dispatch strategy to flatten demand spikes
Full load calculations and technical documentation
